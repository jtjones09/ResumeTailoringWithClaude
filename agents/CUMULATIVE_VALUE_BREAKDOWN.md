## INTERVIEW Q&A PREP

**Q: "That seems high - can you really defend $100M?"**
**A:** "Let me walk you through it. At Molex, I delivered $27M in annual revenue recovery - that's documented in financial systems. At Advocate Aurora, over 10 years, I delivered $53M cumulative through initiatives like Dragon Medical ($30M), COVID mobile screening ($12M), and infrastructure optimization ($3.5M). Each of these has financial documentation. So conservatively, I've delivered $80M+, and with other initiatives, $100M+ is defensible."

**Q: "How much of that was your work vs team effort?"**
**A:** "All of these were team efforts - I led the teams and initiatives that delivered these outcomes. For example, with the $27M Molex recovery, the Commercial Excellence team built the analytics, but I directed the solution delivery. For Dragon Medical at AAH, I managed vendor relationships and directed the enterprise rollout. I don't claim I did these alone - I claim I led the teams that delivered these results."

**Q: "Is that cumulative or annual?"**
**A:** "It's cumulative career value. The $27M at Molex is annual recurring. The AAH numbers are cumulative over my 10-year tenure there. I'm very clear about the distinction."

**Q: "Can you show documentation?"**
**A:** "Some of it, yes - I have business cases, vendor reports, and budget documentation I can share where not confidential. Some is in company financial systems I no longer have access to, but I can provide references who can verify these numbers."

## WHAT TO EMPHASIZE

**1. Specificity:**
- Not vague "millions" - exact numbers with sources
- Clear timeframes (annual vs cumulative)
- Documented in financial systems

**2. Leadership:**
- Led teams that delivered outcomes
- Not claiming solo credit
- Directed initiatives, managed vendors, set strategy

**3. Conservatism:**
- $95M is highly documented
- $100M+ includes some estimates
- Only counting what's defensible

**4. Pattern:**
- Consistent value delivery across roles
- Both cost savings and revenue generation
- Healthcare, manufacturing, consulting

---

## RED FLAGS TO AVOID

❌ "I personally delivered..." (sounds like sole credit)
✅ "I led the team that delivered..."

❌ "Approximately $100M" (vague)
✅ "$100M+ cumulative, with $95M highly documented"

❌ Including unimplemented projections
✅ Only counting delivered outcomes

❌ Claiming accuracy to the dollar
✅ "Approximately, based on financial documentation"
