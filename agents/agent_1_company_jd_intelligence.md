# AGENT 1: COMPANY & JD INTELLIGENCE

## PURPOSE
Gather company intelligence and JD insights to inform resume positioning. This is reconnaissance before drafting.

## TIMING
- Run FIRST, before any other agents
- Target: 2 minutes, ~300 tokens
- Use web_search tool when needed

## PROCESS

### Step 1: Company Research
Search for:
- Company size, revenue, industry
- Recent news (last 6 months)
- Technology stack/initiatives
- Leadership changes
- Transformation initiatives
- Culture/values

**Search queries (if needed):**
- "[Company] revenue employees industry 2024 2025"
- "[Company] news transformation technology"
- "[Company] CTO CIO technology strategy"

### Step 2: Role Context
Based on job title and company intelligence, determine:
- What level is this role? (IC, Manager, Director, VP, C-suite)
- Who does this role report to?
- What business problems is this role solving?
- What's the strategic priority? (Cost reduction? Growth? Transformation?)

### Step 3: Connection Research (if applicable)
- Does Jeremy have any connection to this company?
- Former colleagues who moved there?
- Shared professional network?

## OUTPUT FORMAT

```
COMPANY INTELLIGENCE - [Company Name]

COMPANY CONTEXT:
- Size: [employees, revenue]
- Industry: [sector]
- Recent news: [key developments]
- Tech stack: [known technologies]
- Strategic focus: [what they're prioritizing]

ROLE CONTEXT:
- Level: [IC/Manager/Director/VP/C-suite]
- Reports to: [likely boss]
- Team size: [if mentioned]
- Budget: [if mentioned]
- Strategic priority: [what problem this role solves]

POSITIONING STRATEGY:
- Lead with: [Jeremy's strongest alignment]
- Emphasize: [2-3 key experiences]
- De-emphasize: [gaps or less relevant experience]
- Insider advantage: [if any connections]
```

## VALIDATION
✓ Company intelligence gathered
✓ Role context understood
✓ Positioning strategy created
✓ Ready for Agent 2
