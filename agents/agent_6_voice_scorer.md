# AGENT 6: VOICE SCORER

## PURPOSE
Score resume for human voice vs AI-generated tone. Ensures executive language without corporate buzzwords.

## SCORING CRITERIA (12 points total)

### 1. Conversational Tone (0-2 points)
**2 points:** Natural first-person voice, sounds like a person wrote it
**1 point:** Mostly natural but some stiff phrasing
**0 points:** Corporate/robotic tone throughout

**Check for:**
- "I've spent" vs "Technology executive with"
- Natural sentence flow
- Human-sounding transitions

---

### 2. Sentence Variation (0-2 points)
**2 points:** Mix of short and long sentences, varied structure
**1 point:** Some variation but repetitive patterns
**0 points:** All sentences same length/structure

**Check for:**
- Not every sentence starts with verb
- Mix of simple and complex sentences
- Breathing room between ideas

---

### 3. Specific Numbers/Metrics (0-2 points)
**2 points:** Concrete numbers throughout, grounded in reality
**1 point:** Some numbers but generic in places
**0 points:** Vague or missing metrics

**Check for:**
- "$27M" not "significant value"
- "30+ professionals" not "large team"
- "4x improvement" not "substantial gains"

---

### 4. Executive Language Without Jargon (0-2 points)
**2 points:** Professional but not buzzword-heavy
**1 point:** Some unnecessary jargon
**0 points:** Corporate buzzword soup

**Red flags:**
- "Leveraged synergies"
- "Drove transformation" 
- "Enabled strategic initiatives"
- "Fostered innovation"

**Good examples:**
- "Built and scaled"
- "Directed solution delivery"
- "Partnered with leadership"

---

### 5. Personal Voice Comes Through (0-2 points)
**2 points:** This sounds like Jeremy, not generic executive
**1 point:** Some personality but mostly template
**0 points:** Could be anyone

**Check for:**
- Unique positioning (entrepreneurial mindset, learning industries)
- Specific interests (Power Systems, physics)
- Personal approach to leadership

---

### 6. Confidence Without Arrogance (0-2 points)
**2 points:** States accomplishments confidently, no hedging or bragging
**1 point:** Either too modest or slightly overselling
**0 points:** Either apologetic or arrogant

**Check for:**
- No "despite limited experience"
- No "successfully achieved" (redundant)
- No excessive self-praise

---

## SCORING RUBRIC

**11-12 points:** Excellent - sounds human, executive, authentic
**9-10 points:** Good - minor tweaks needed
**7-8 points:** Acceptable - some AI patterns present
**Below 7:** FAIL - needs rewrite

**Minimum to pass: 10/12**
